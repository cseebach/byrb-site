Running Corps Classes

Premium Running Endurance

The Running Corps endurance training approach is based upon a simple premise: If you want to become a better runner, you must begin by building a better running body. The Premium Running Endurance classes target all the individual components of your running body: your muscles, cardiovascular system, nervous system, connective tissue, and more. The goal is better endurance, improved performance, and injury prevention. During two sessions per week, you’ll train intervals, tempo, hill work, form drills, plyometrics, resistance training, and the latest stretching techniques. Each participant receives a 12-week training schedule (with distance and pace for non-class workouts). Cost is $275 for 24 sessions. Workouts led by Coach Pete Magill. Location: South Pasadena High School.

Runner 360

The Runner 360 is a high-powered series of workouts designed to create maximum running fitness in three categories: speed, strength, and skills. Developed to improve the running ability of all athletes (whether your goal is distance running, basketball, football, soccer, all-around fitness, or any other), these classes offer an exhilarating and challenging mix of HIIT (high-intensity interval training), form drills, body-strengthening exercises, plyometrics, running circuits, and more. This high-energy workout is perfect for anyone who wishes they were faster, stronger, or more explosive. Workouts led by Coach Pete Magill. Cost is $120 for 6 sessions. Location: South Pasadena High School.
